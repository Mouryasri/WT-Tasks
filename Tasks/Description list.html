Course Catalog

Mathematics
A study of numbers, quantities, and shapes, including algebra, geometry, and calculus.
Computer Science
The study of the theory, design, and implementation of computer systems and algorithms.
English Literature
An exploration of literary works from around the world, including novels, poetry, and drama.
Biology
The study of living organisms, including their structure, function, and evolution.
History
An examination of past events, people, and cultures, including ancient civilizations and modern societies.
Foreign Languages
A study of languages such as Spanish, French, Mandarin, and Arabic, including grammar, vocabulary, and culture.
Physics
The study of the natural world, including the laws of motion, energy, and the behavior of matter and energy.
Psychology
The scientific study of behavior and mental processes, including cognition, emotion, and social behavior.
